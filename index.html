How It Works

Play these five daily games in order. Press Next Game after each one so your score and progress are saved.

Memory Game

Flip cards and match identical pairs. Complete all pairs in the fewest moves and shortest time.

Reaction Game

React quickly to incoming cues and avoid missing targets. Faster and more accurate responses give higher scores.

Logic Puzzle

Solve each puzzle using pattern logic. Fewer incorrect moves and quicker completion improve your result.

Math Challenge

Answer mental math questions under time pressure. Correct answers build your score and streak.

Pattern Matching

Identify the missing shape in each sequence and choose the correct tile to complete the pattern.
